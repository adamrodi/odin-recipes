Beef Burrito Bowl

Description

Get ready to enjoy a flavorful and easy-to-make Beef Burrito Bowl! This recipe combines juicy, seasoned beef with fresh veggies and rice, all topped with cheese, lettuce, sour cream, and your favorites. Perfect for a quick dinner or meal prep, this dish is nutritious, customizable, and absolutely delicious. Dive into this satisfying meal today!

Ingredients

  • Extra Lean (93% or higher) Ground Beef
  • White or Brown Rice
  • Shredded Lettuce
  • Fat-Free Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Yellow Onion
  • Garlic cloves
  • Taco Seasoning

Steps

Cook Ground Beef

  1. Begin by dicing a yellow onion and garlic. Then heat and grease a large pot/pan and start cooking the vegetables.
  2. Once veggies are slightly browned add in the ground beef and turn the heat to high. Add taco seasoning to the pan.
  3. Mix and chop the mixture into the beef is broken into small pieces. Continue mixing throughout the cooking process to ensure even cooking.
  4. When ready scoop out a small portion of beed for taste testing. When cooled enough to eat try the beef and add seasoning if needed. Turn off heat once fully cooked.

Cook Rice

  1. Add 1 cup of rice and 1.5 cups of water to a pot.
  2. Bring to simmer on medium-high heat.
  3. Turn heat to low, cover with lid, and cook for 12 - 14 minutes.
  4. Let it rest for 10 minutes then fluff.

Combine and Enjoy!

  1. Put a layer of rice in a bowl, then beef mixture on top.
  2. Put as much cheese, lettuce, sour cream, hot sauce, any any other toppings as you like.
  3. Enjoy. (Mandatory!)
