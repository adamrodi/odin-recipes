Protein Chicken Pasta

Description

Protein chicken pasta is a quick, easy, and delicious meal. It is perfect for a low-calorie, high-protein option that can be prepared in 20 to 30 minutes, and enjoyed throughout the week. This dish consists of juicy tender chicken breasts, with a creamy alfredo sauce, and penne protein pasta. This pairs wonderfully with garlic or cheesey bread.

Ingredients

  • Banza Penne Protein Pasta Noodles
  • Chicken Breasts
  • Canned or Homemade Alfredo Sauce
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Fresh Thyme
  • Yellow Onion
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesean Cheese
  • Cooking Oil

Steps

Prep and Cook Chicken

  1. Mince onions and garlic
  2. Prepare chicken with seasoning of your choice (Breasts can be left whole or cut into smaller pieces)
  3. Heat pan on the stove using medium heat
  4. Grease pan with cooling oil
  5. Add seasoned chicken breasts to the pan
  6. Add minced garlic and onions to the pan with the chicken breasts
  7. Flip the chicken when bottom side is seared: golden brown color
  8. Add a twig of thyme and a tablespoon of butter to the pan
  9. Baste the melted butter onto the chicken with a spoon
  10. When chicken is cooked through (internal temperature of 165Â°F) transfer to a clean cutting board
  11. Discard thyme and turn off heat, saving the pan for a later step

Cook Pasta Noodles

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a rolling boil
  2. Add noodles, stir, and reduce to a simmer
  3. Cook to desired firmness, stirring throughout, about 6-8 minutes
  4. Strain and rinse with water

Combine all Ingredients

  1. Reheat and grease the saved pan (low to medium heat)
  2. Add alfredo sauce and noodles to the pan and stir
  3. Reseason to your liking (optional)
  4. Add parmesean and mozzarella and stir
  5. Once heated, dish the pasta and add cooked chicken along with more parmesean
