Protein Chicken Pasta

Description

Protein chicken pasta is a quick, easy, and delicious meal. It is perfect for a low-calorie, high-protein option that can be prepared in 20 to 30 minutes, and enjoyed throughout the week. This dish consists of juicy tender chicken breasts, with a homemade fettuccini sauce, and penne protein pasta. This pairs wonderfully with garlic bread or cheesey bread.
